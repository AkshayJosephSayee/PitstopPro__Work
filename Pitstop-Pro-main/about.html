About Pitstop Pro

Pitstop Pro – Complete Car Care Services Pitstop Pro is a professional, multi-brand car servicing solution designed to make vehicle maintenance convenient, transparent, and reliable. Services are delivered either at partner garages or directly at your doorstep by trained technicians, using high-quality OEM or equivalent parts. Key Services Offered Periodic Maintenance Service (PMS): Oil changes, filters, fluids, and general inspection to keep your car in top condition. Mechanical & Electrical Repairs: Engine tuning, brake overhauls, suspension repairs, battery replacement, and electrical troubleshooting. Diagnostics & Health Check: Computerized scanning to detect issues early and avoid costly breakdowns. AC Service & Repairs: Cooling system cleaning, refrigerant refill, and component replacement. Tire & Wheel Care: Wheel alignment, balancing, tire replacement, and puncture repair. Detailing & Cleaning: Interior vacuuming, exterior wash, polishing, and protective coatings. Roadside Assistance: Emergency breakdown support and towing. Why Pitstop Pro?
